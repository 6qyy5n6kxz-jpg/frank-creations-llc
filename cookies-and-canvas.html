What to Expect

Easy age guidance, simple refreshments, and a host-friendly flow

The strongest official details are practical ones: who can attend, when parents may need to help, what refreshments are included, and how the event stays fun without getting chaotic.

Age guidance

Children and adults of all ages are welcome. Younger children may need help from their parents, while school-age kids often follow along on their own.

Refreshment policy

Cookies come with each registration, snacks are welcome, and unlike Wine & Canvas events, alcoholic beverages are never served at Cookies & Canvas.

Best-fit occasions

Birthday parties, school family nights, libraries, camps, youth groups, community venues, and kid-focused special events are the strongest fit.
